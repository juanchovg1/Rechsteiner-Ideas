Major Inspection Types

Forms
1

Required Documents

Permit documents (approved building permit), Site plans indicating formwork layout, Engineering or architectural drawings for foundation.

Common Reasons for Inspection Failures

Inaccurate or incomplete formwork installation, Incorrect dimensions or alignment, Insufficient structural support or bracing.

Slab Poly (Where Required)
2

Required Documents

Building permit, Slab detail drawings showing vapor barrier placement, Material specifications for vapor barrier.

Common Reasons for Inspection Failures

Improper installation or torn vapor barrier, Overlapping seams not properly sealed, Missing vapor barrier in required areas.
